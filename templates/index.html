Mamal Computer Lab Dashboard

How to Use the Dashboard

  • Upload CSV File: Click "Upload CSV" to load your student data.
  • Filter Data: Use the filters to narrow down the data.
  • Apply Filters: Click "Apply Filters" to apply your selections.
  • Load More Data: Click "Load More" to display additional records.
  • Download Data: Export the data by clicking "Download CSV" or "Download PDF."
  • Delete Data: Click "Delete Data" to clear the table.
  • Refresh Page: Click "Refresh" to reload the page.

Note: If you're filtering between two dates, keep in mind that the data starts from the next day. For example, if you want data from April 3rd, 2025, filter from April 2nd, 2025.
